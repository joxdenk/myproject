Liver Aid Foundation(LAF)

"Liver Is Life"

Brief Background

  • LAF is a non-profit organization that promotes liver health and liver disease prevention and awareness

Our Vision

  • Our vision is to create a world free from liver diseases.

Mission Statement

  • The mission of LAF is to advocate, facilitate and promote education, support and research for the prevention, treatment and cure of liver diseases

Core Values

  • These values guid our work as voluntees and staffs of LAF as we move towards our ultimate goal of a world without liver diseases. By living these values everyday, we offer hope to all those affected by liver disease.

1. Leadership

  • We are leaders in the fight against liver diseases. We advance the interests of people affected by liver disease through individual and team achievements. We welcome opportunities for change, growth and colloboration. New ideas and creative solutions are encouraged and we are willing to take calculated risk.

2. Intergrity

  • We are honest and and striaghtforward in all that we do.We act in good faith, deliveron our promise and commitmentsand are accountable for our actions. We act responibly with resources entrusted on us.
